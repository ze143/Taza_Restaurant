Our History

Taza Restaurant was founded in 2015 by a team of passionate chefs and gastronomers. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable dining experience that caters to the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers. With over 200 restaurants serving around the globe, Taza Restaurant continues to grow and evolve, always striving to create a unique and memorable dining experience.
